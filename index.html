Our Mission

Skills and Innovation Academy (SIA) is committed to preparing young minds for a rapidly changing future. SIA is an academy where children are taught skills in AI Literacy, Generative AI, Prompt Engineering, Vibe Coding, and Green Skills to drive innovation. Children will be the architects of their lives. Our learners will be equipped with skills such as creativity, critical thinking, and problem solving.
